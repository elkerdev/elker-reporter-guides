Getting Started

When submitting a report through Elker:

  • Your data is protected throughout the process
  • All communications are encrypted and secure
  • You can check on your report status at any time using your report ID and chosen login method
  • You can communicate with your contact point while remaining anonymous

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues or have questions not covered in these guides please email Elker support at support@elker.com
